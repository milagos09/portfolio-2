About Me

Western Visayas College of Science and Technology

I graduated from Western Visayas College of Science and Technology, presently known as Iloilo Science and Technology University, with a degree in Electronics Engineering.

But I discovered my passion for technology through my ICT subjects. Despite my uncertainty at the time, I earned my license as an electronics engineer after graduation. However, my interest in software engineering grew as the years went by.

Accenture

I'm an electronics engineer turned software engineer with a specialization in Node.js. I recently joined Accenture and have been working here for less than a year. Despite being new to software engineering, I'm passionate about creating robust applications and have found my experience in electronics engineering to be invaluable in problem-solving and analytical skills.

Accenture's innovative and supportive environment has allowed me to continue growing and learning in my new role as a software engineer. I'm excited to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology and advance my career in software engineering at Accenture.
